1. What we store

All core resume data you enter in the app (work history, education, skills, projects, summary) is stored locally on your device only.

2. What happens when you upload a PDF

When you upload a PDF file only, the app extracts plain text locally:

  • First extracts email addresses and phone numbers and removes/masks them locally
  • Remaining content used for manual form filling
  • No AI processing, no servers involved

3. PDF Text Limitations

App extracts plain text only from PDF. Paragraphs, spacing, tables, or formatting are not retained. You can reorganize content in forms after extraction.

4. Your control

Everything stays local. No consent needed for external processing. Review extracted text before use.

5. Data Deletion

  • Local Data: Use 'Delete Profile' button or uninstall app
