Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Welcome to NMPrompt. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle information in our app.

1. Information We Collect

NMPrompt is a utility app for prompts and text tools. We do not require regular users to create an account. The Login feature is strictly for Administrative use only.

2. Features and Usage Tracking

  • Prompts & Text: Users can view, search, and copy prompts.
  • Copy Counting: We track how many times each prompt is copied. This is used solely to show users which prompts are "Trending" or most popular. This data is anonymous.
  • Image & Text Services: Processing is done only to provide the requested prompts.
  • Admin Login: Credentials are collected only for admin authentication.

3. Third-Party Advertising (Ads)

Our app uses third-party advertising services (like Google AdMob). These services may collect device identifiers to show relevant ads to you.

4. Data Security

We use standard measures to protect the limited data we process. We do not collect or sell personal user information.

5. Children's Privacy

NMPrompt does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you have concerns, please contact us.
