1. Introduction

Welcome to Tej Vidya. This is an educational app designed to share and read study notes. By using our app, you agree to our privacy policy and terms.

2. Privacy Policy

  • Account Data: We use Firebase Authentication to safely manage your login details.
  • App Data: Notes, images, and quizzes you share are stored securely in Google Firebase.

  • Account & Data Deletion: You can request to permanently delete your account and all associated data by going to Settings > Advanced Settings in the app, or by emailing us directly at nitemoraix@gmail.com.

3. Terms & Conditions (User Generated Content Policy)

To keep the app safe for all students, you must strictly follow these rules regarding User Generated Content (UGC):

  • Educational Use Only: You can create, share, and delete your own study notes. Uploading copyrighted material that doesn't belong to you is strictly prohibited.
  • Zero Tolerance Policy: We have a zero-tolerance policy for abusive, violent, sexual, or illegal content.
  • Reporting & Moderation: Users can flag/report any inappropriate post. Our admin team reviews reports and will delete offending content immediately.
  • Permanent Ban: If you violate these rules, your account will be permanently banned and removed from the app forever without prior notice.
